Japan National Volleyball Team

Japan plays so fast, and they’re crazy good at saving the ball. Yūki Ishikawa is my favorite to watch because he always comes through in those clutch moments. I’ll never forget when they beat Brazil in the 2021 Tokyo Olympics; it showed me how smart gameplay can beat pure strength.

Philippines National Volleyball Team

The Philippines team plays with so much hype. Their win against Egypt in the 2025 Men’s World Championship made me proud because it was our first-ever win on the men’s world stage, and it happened right here at home.

Brazil National Volleyball Team

Brazil has been one of the strongest teams ever. Their comeback in the 2016 Rio Olympics final, when they won gold at home, was so insane. That’s why they’ll always be seen as one of the greatest teams ever in my eyes.
