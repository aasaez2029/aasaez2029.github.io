Why I Love Volleyball

Volleyball is my favorite sport because it’s fun and gives me a rush of adrenaline. At training, it improves how I play and what I decide to do next. As for the actual matches, I love playing and watching practice matches until 6 pm. I love the feeling of a clean spike or a long rally, especially when everyone is locked in on scoring your team's point.

Rules & Mechanics

Rules

  • 6 players per team, rotating clockwise after each serve won.
  • Each team can hit the ball up to 3 times before sending it back.
  • The ball must stay within court lines to be valid.
  • Points are scored when the ball lands on the opponent’s court or they commit an error.
  • Violations include touching the net, double contact, or stepping over the line during serve.

Mechanics

  • Play begins with a serve from behind the end line.
  • Teams rally until one side fails to return the ball properly.
  • Main sequence: bump → set → spike.
  • Rotation ensures each player serves and covers front and back row positions.
  • Specialized roles: setter, libero, blockers, and hitters.
